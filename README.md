# 🏦 Banking Analytics Dashboard | Python + Power BI

A comprehensive Banking Analytics Dashboard built using **Power BI**, **Python**, and **DAX** to analyze customer banking behavior, loans, deposits, credit cards, and business lending. The project includes complete **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** in Python followed by an interactive Power BI dashboard for business decision-making.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This project transforms raw banking customer data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and advanced analytics.

The dashboard helps banking professionals monitor:

- Customer Portfolio
- Loan Performance
- Deposit Analysis
- Credit Card Utilization
- Business Lending
- Customer Segmentation
- Banking Relationship Analysis

# 📈 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

EDA was performed using **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)**.

### ✔ Univariate Analysis

- Customer distribution
- Nationality
- Banking Relationship
- Gender
- Occupation
- Loyalty Classification
- Fee Structure

---

### ✔ Bivariate Analysis

Analyzed relationships between customer attributes such as:

- Nationality vs Banking Products
- Gender vs Banking Relationship
- Occupation vs Customer Segments
- Fee Structure vs Nationality

---

### ✔ Numerical Analysis

Distribution analysis of

- Estimated Income
- Superannuation Savings
- Credit Card Balance
- Bank Loans
- Bank Deposits
- Checking Accounts
- Savings Accounts

Histogram and KDE plots were used to understand data distribution and skewness.

---

### ✔ Correlation Analysis

Generated a correlation heatmap to identify relationships among numerical variables.

Key observations:

- Moderate correlation between Estimated Income and Superannuation Savings
- Positive relationship between Income and Loan Amount
- Weak correlations among most banking products indicating diversified customer behavior

- European customers contribute the highest loan and deposit amounts.
- Medium-income customers account for the largest share of banking products.
- Private Banking relationship customers hold the highest loan portfolio.
- Business Lending represents a significant portion of total lending.
- Deposit concentration varies across nationalities.
- Customer engagement enables deeper segmentation of banking products.
